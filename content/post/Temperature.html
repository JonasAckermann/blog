The Urban Observatory is a collection of sensors deployed in Newcastle Upon Tyne, measuring weather and environment, traffic and parking and tidal and river level. Let’s first consider temperature data located around St James’ Park, there is a high density of sensors here and temperature is easy to identify faulty sensors, since we have familiarity with the type of data.
